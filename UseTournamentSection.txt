# Guide: How to Upload Tournament Content

This guide explains how to upload and manage content in the Tournament section of the MKTA Tennis Academy website, including images, results, and schedules.

## Tournament Images

### Uploading New Tournament Images

1. **Access the Tournament Admin Panel**:
   - Log in with your administrator account
   - Navigate to Tournament Section

2. **Upload Images**:
   - Click on "Manage Tournament Images" button
   - Click "Upload New Image"
   - Fill in the form:
     * Title (required): Enter a descriptive title for the image
     * Description (optional): Add details about the image
     * Image File: Click "Browse" and select a JPG, JPEG, or PNG file (max size: 5MB)
   - Click "Upload" button
   - The image will be processed and added to the tournament gallery

3. **Image Requirements**:
   - Recommended size: 1200x800 pixels
   - Supported formats: JPG, JPEG, PNG
   - Maximum file size: 5MB

### Managing Existing Images

1. **View All Images**:
   - On the Tournament Admin Panel, click "Manage Tournament Images"
   - All uploaded images will be displayed with their details

2. **Edit Image Details**:
   - Click on the "Edit" button next to an image
   - Update title, description, or replace the image file
   - Click "Save Changes"

3. **Delete Images**:
   - Click on the "Delete" button next to an image
   - Confirm deletion when prompted
   - Note: Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone

## Tournament Results

### Adding Tournament Results

1. **Access Results Management**:
   - Navigate to Tournament Section
   - Click on "Manage Results" button

2. **Add New Result**:
   - Click "Add New Result" button
   - Complete the form with:
     * Tournament ID: Select from the dropdown menu
     * Category: Enter the tournament category (e.g., "Men's Singles")
     * Winner: Enter the name of the winner
     * Runner-up: Enter the name of the runner-up
     * Score: Enter the match score (e.g., "6-4, 6-3")
     * Date: Select the date of the final match
   - Click "Save Result" button

3. **Edit Existing Results**:
   - Click "Edit" next to any result
   - Update the fields as needed
   - Click "Save Changes"

4. **Delete Results**:
   - Click "Delete" next to the result you want to remove
   - Confirm deletion when prompted

## Tournament Schedule

### Managing Tournament Schedule

1. **Access Schedule Management**:
   - Navigate to Tournament Section
   - Click on "Manage Schedule" button

2. **Add Schedule Entry**:
   - Click "Add New Schedule" button
   - Complete the form:
     * Date: Select the date of the matches
     * Time: Enter the starting time
     * Category: Enter the tournament category
     * Round: Enter the round (e.g., "Quarter-final", "Semi-final")
     * Court: Enter the court number or name
     * Players: Enter the players' names (e.g., "John Smith vs. Alex Johnson")
   - Click "Save Schedule" button

3. **Edit Schedule Entry**:
   - Click "Edit" next to any schedule entry
   - Update the fields as needed
   - Click "Save Changes"

4. **Delete Schedule Entry**:
   - Click "Delete" next to the entry you want to remove
   - Confirm deletion when prompted

## Best Practices

1. **Image Optimization**:
   - Compress images before uploading for faster loading
   - Use descriptive titles and alt text for accessibility
   - Maintain consistent image dimensions (aspect ratio)

2. **Data Organization**:
   - Keep tournament data organized by using consistent naming conventions
   - Group related tournaments together
   - Archive old tournament data periodically

3. **Regular Updates**:
   - Update tournament schedules promptly
   - Post results as soon as possible after matches
   - Refresh gallery images to keep content engaging
